>> COVER ANALYSIS

The cover depicts a surrealist interpretation of "The Face on Mars," a popular topic during the early 90s following the Viking orbiter images. The artwork suggests a biomechanical origin, hinting at the magazine's focus on the intersection of biology and extraterrestrial technology.

>> ISSUE CONTENTS

This issue contains the seminal article "Whispers from the Void," which detailed the first public release of SETI signal data. It also features a rare interview with Whitley Strieber regarding the reception of "Communion" five years later.
